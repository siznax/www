Some things I am thinking about ...
  • Democracy is increasingly under threat
  • Most of us live and work on stolen land whose people are still here
  • Many of us benefit from and unwittingly perpetuate systemic racism
  • We have detonated over 2,100 nuclear bombs on land, sea, air, and space
  • Nuclear weapons testing is associated with leukaemia and cancer
  • We have lost 70% of wild animals in the last fifty years
  • Humans and farm animals now make up 90% of mammals, 70% of birds
  • In 2019 we passed a climate tipping point of 350ppm C02 (now 415ppm)
  • Globally 80% of biodiversity is now only on vulnerable indigenous land
  • Clear-cutting, strip-mining, and mountaintop removal continue unabated
  • Only about 9% of plastic gets recycled, we dump or burn the rest
  • We dump 8.8 million tons of plastic into the oceans each year
  • Almost all sea birds contain plastic
  • Soon there will be more plastic than fish in the oceans
  • Electric cars are built and charged by burning fossil fuels
  • Solar panels require mining and burning toxic heavy minerals not "sand"
  • Earth has had at least five major ice ages and two snowball earths
  • Birds are dinosaurs which have not gone extinct
